About Us

We are a family owned business proudly serving the city of North Chicago for over 30 years! Hunan Sixth Happiness is committed to make every visit a welcoming experience with warm and piping hot Chinese food.

From our kitchen to your table, Hunan Sixth Happiness uses fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes to bring you a taste of tradition. We are grateful to serve you our dishes and we look forward to sharing our family's hospitality with you!
